## Day 1 — Arrival Phuket & Yacht Introduction

Arrive Phuket International Airport. Transfer to Ao Chalong yacht marina. Board fleet of 42ft mono-hull sailing yachts (2 students per yacht + skipper). Afternoon yacht familiarisation: safety briefing, rigging, sail controls, helmet and life jacket fitting. Evening marina dinner. Skipper-led introduction to the Andaman route.

## Day 2 — Sail to Koh Racha Yai

Morning departure under engine, raise sails once clear of the bay. Students rotate through crew roles — helm, jib sheet, main trim. Afternoon arrive Koh Racha Yai. Snorkel stop at the southern reef (shallow coral garden, parrotfish, moray eels). Evening on anchor — navigation exercise using paper charts and GPS, knot-tying workshop.

## Day 3 — Koh Haa & Koh Bida Nai

Morning sail to the Koh Haa islands (1.5 hours). Two snorkel stops — Koh Haa lagoon and the chimney dive site. Afternoon navigation passage to Koh Bida Nai. Biology flash-talk on Andaman coral spawning events. Evening on anchor — group logbook entries, skipper assessment of student helm time.

## Day 4 — Koh Phi Phi Ley & Maya Bay

Morning sail to Koh Phi Phi Ley. Guided walk to Maya Bay — discussion on the 2018 closure, coral recovery data, and marine park management. Afternoon snorkel at Pileh Lagoon. Evening overnight at Koh Phi Don — beach BBQ, night navigation walk.

## Day 5 — Koh Lanta & Krabi Coast

Morning motor-sail to Koh Lanta Yai island. Land visit to the old town — Sino-Portuguese architecture walk, local seafood lunch. Afternoon beach clean-up activity on Klong Dao beach (service hours logged). Evening return to yacht, sunset sail toward Koh Jum.

## Day 6 — Koh Yao Islands & Certification

Morning short hop to Koh Yao Yai. Students complete their ASA 101 sailing certification elements — docking, anchoring, crew overboard recovery under skipper supervision. Afternoon snorkel at Koh Yao Yai's eastern shore. Final evening dinner as a fleet — student presentations on their favourite anchorage.

## Day 7 — Return to Phuket & Departure

Morning sail back to Ao Chalong marina (2-3 hours). Depart yachts after equipment debrief and logbook sign-off. Transfer to Phuket International Airport for onward travel.

## Curriculum fit

- **IB MYP Physical & Health Education**: criterion C (application and performance) through practical sailing and water activities
- **IB DP CAS**: activity component — sustained sailing skill development over 7 days
- **IGCSE Physical Education CIE**: demonstrates physical literacy in an alternative sport environment
- **A-Level Geography**: individual investigation option on marine ecosystem recovery post-closure (Maya Bay case study)

## Safety

RYA-certified skipper aboard each yacht at all times. Coast Guard VHF monitoring. Life raft, EPIRB, and first-aid kit on every vessel. All students complete a swim test on Day 1 (minimum 50m). Wetsuits and snorkel gear fitted before departure. Medical history reviewed before booking. Weather forecasts checked twice daily by skipper.

## What's included

7 days aboard 42ft sailing yacht (2 students per cabin, shared heads), skipper fees, ASA certification materials and assessment, snorkel equipment, dinghy and kayak use, all meals aboard and marina dinners, park entry fees, service activity materials, marine biology flash-talk.
