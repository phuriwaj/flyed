## Why flyed runs Koh Tao trips

We partnered with a small, instructor-owned dive school on Koh Tao in 2019 after previous operators gave us inconsistent teaching quality. Our dive school holds PADI 5-star status and has a strong safety record. All instructors are English-speaking and experienced with teenage learners. We run a maximum of 8 students per instructor to ensure quality and safety in the water.

## What students do

- PADI Open Water theory and pool skills with our partner dive school
- Four open-water dives at different reef sites around Koh Tao
- Marine biology session: coral identification, fish species recording, and impact assessment
- Night dive experience at a shallow reef site
- Island orientation: hike to the island's Buddhist temple and viewpoint

## Logistics

- Best months: January–May and November–December (flat seas, good visibility)
- Monsoon Gulf: June–October — rough seas and reduced visibility; dive schools may limit open-water training
- Minimum age: 15 years for PADI Open Water; we run a surface snorkel program for younger groups
- Visa: standard 30-day exemption
- Travel: overnight train or bus from Bangkok to Chumphon (5–8 hours), then 2-hour ferry to Koh Tao; or domestic flight to Chumphon followed by ferry
