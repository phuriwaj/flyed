## Why flyed runs Krabi trips

Our Krabi program launched in 2019 as an extension of our Phuket marine offerings. We added rock climbing after teacher feedback that groups wanted more challenge-based adventure. Our Krabi climbing guide is a local climber who has established routes across Railay and Tonsai. For marine work, we partner with a Krabi-based environmental group running seagrass and mangrove monitoring.

## What students do

- Sea-kayak exploration through Krabi's mangrove channels with a marine-ecology guide
- Rock climbing at Railay Beach with certified instructors, suited to all ability levels
- Seagrass-meadow survey fieldwork — students count and measure indicator species
- Sunset hike to Tiger Cave Temple with panoramic Andaman views
- Koh Lanta island day trip for reef snorkeling with coral-health recording

## Logistics

- Best months: November–April (dry, flat seas)
- Monsoon: May–October — wind and swells affect boat trips; climbing remains viable year-round
- Visa: standard 30-day exemption
- Travel: domestic flight to Krabi Airport; speedboat from Phuket takes 2–3 hours
