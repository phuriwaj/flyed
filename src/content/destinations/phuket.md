## Why flyed runs Phuket trips

We started Phuket programs in 2017, originally built around snorkeling tours. Over time we deepened the marine-science component, partnering with a Phuket marine research station that monitors coral bleaching and runs community reef-monitoring programs. Our Phuket trips combine real data collection with adventure activities that keep the energy high.

## What students do

- Reef-survey fieldwork: students contribute actual coral-cover data to a local monitoring database
- Mangrove kayak tour with a marine-ecology brief on nursery habitat function
- Open-water snorkeling at two different reef sites
- Rock climbing at Rawai with certified instructors
- Thai cooking class at a beachside school in old Phuket town

## Logistics

- Best months: November–April (dry, calm seas)
- Monsoon: May–October — Andaman coast is windy and some dive sites close; we run modified programs
- Sea conditions: November–April flat seas; May–October swells on west coast
- Visa: standard 30-day exemption
- Travel: direct international flights to Phuket Airport; 1-hour domestic from Bangkok
